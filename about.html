Message from our Chairman.

Dear All,

I am delighted to share that Kanchanjunga English Secondary School is among a few national benchmark institutions known for quality and cutting-edge education established to shape our children as autonomous learners distinguished at the strength of 21st century skills we make an attempt to instill. Our team effort at Kanchanjunga is to ensure the most conducive learning environment together building education within a holistic learning framework and systems which can be idealized to enhancing 21st century skills for these smart generations to live and thrive through emerging real-life changes and challenges. We are persistently working in the field of education to uplift the level of our education system and its quality as key to determine learning acumen and caliber in every child the school values to be important. that Kanchanjunga English Secondary School has highly structured resource centers lending students a plentiful access of most updated resources, assets and equipment deemed essential to design learning as an ongoing process at all levels so as to allow our communities achieve the best not only in subject-focused study but also in honing best skills the learners will need to use in real situations on multiple purposes.
We envision imparting education made compatible with international standards. In addition to the Kanchanjunga academic system embracing modern dynamics featuring input, process and output levels, our curriculum is enriched with a blend of national and international academic and skill support programs. It is on the interactive spectrum that builds a constructive pedagogy that we use to collaborate with some of the finest international educational academies. Our persistent efforts in building education upon skills of collaboration have helped us revise and reform our policy that thereby enables Kanchanjunga to design curriculum made instrumental to catering our young generations with value added education approached in new perspectives on developing both local and global citizenship character in our children.
For educational processes and services at Kanchanjunga appropriated to meet national standard, the school is operated in the fine-built infrastructure with plenty of space that the school uses for a repertoire of sports and other outdoor curricula which as could be understood are significant for holistic and comprehensive growth of our children. Set upon the state-of-the-art quality infrastructure, Kanchanjunga School assures commendable presentable teaching-learning facilities, resources, technologies and these entail; well-equipped library with volumes of resourceful books on different subjects and also e-books, technology enhanced computer lab with unlimited access to internet, online and digital sources, science laboratory for an exposure to practical learning, students readers club and performing arts, and many more... The school has ample space accommodating football ground, cricket pitch and other sports venues where students of all levels are provided a platform to grow with sports outlined.
Most importantly, we have professionally qualified and trained teachers, who are skilled in teaching within the design of mentoring children with the best of their expertise. At the virtue of the blended systems of LRPA ( Learning Reinforcement Practice Application) I believe Kanchanjunga has progressed fast helping our communities of learners and parents attain desired educational results manifested in the best performance our students have shown to us consistently as ever.
Lastly I thank you all for choosing and entrusting us with full confidence to train and educate your child and would take a pleasure to welcome all aspirant members to Kanchanjunga School in order to materialize your unparalleled academic venture to move further with better educational outcomes.
Warm Regards,
Khagendra Prasad Ojha

Message from our Principal.

Dear Communities of Students, Parents and All,
Kanchanjunga English Secondary School, within 3 decades, has grown up impressively in its stature and influence. As a progressive educational institution designed to nurture 21st century citizens, we at Kanchanjunga embrace and implement valuable learning. Kanchanjunga School creates a positive attitude and inspires the students to achieve excellence in their scholarly endeavors embodied in approaches we explore to creating autonomous learning engagements in real actions. A need to adapt to new concepts, content, skills and strategies at different levels for education to advance with innovation is a continuous process and at Kanchanjunga we appreciate this opportunity as we believe it brings out the best in helping us strive further while providing valued learning at all times. We are motivated to remain innovative with our best education level, and round the clock engagements of our professionally trained team and faculty in building our students with excellence achieved in better experiences.
Driven with integrative approaches for holistic education, we at Kanchanjunga School make endeavors on delivering quality education in practice of LRPA module (Learning Reinforcement Practice Application), and our curriculum is vibrant, demanding and progressive. We construct an array of curricula with programs that are in tune with classroom procedures that offer students not only transformational information for solutions but also reinforce their classroom learning in problem-based tasks whereof learning is materialized by students in self-awareness and authentic self-initiated actions. This module defines and involves students as practitioners set to work under the open mentorship of our competent faculty deputed to substantiate education in learner’s active involvement that results from intrinsic motivation. We at Kanchanjunga pledge for an understanding that students are to be educated for the future and it demands an ability in learning to unlearn rudiments replaced by behaviors that will become instrumental to helping individuals create a path for bright future on building innovative and intelligent ideas, skills and strategies. As we embark on our academic journey for the exciting times ahead, we will continue to be co-innovative with our education to ensure growth and ever increasing value by creating an inspiring environment for our students.
Kanchanjunga focuses on research-based independent and practical learning. Our learning method is designed and conducted in a fashion that encompasses multi-literacies sourced and extracted from unfettered use of computer information technology, science and literature to music, theatre, and sports in an integrated framework. Within our system, we believe that by the time students complete their education at Kanchanjunga, they are fully prepared to cope with not only individual, organizational and local social changes but also global challenges of the 21st century. To this aspiration, LRPA module at Kanchanjunga provides a concrete pathway toward higher education for our students to be prepared at the strength of foundational skills the school is committed to enhance.
Kanchanjunga School stands persistent to meeting international standards and is striving to provide it students with unlimited learning exposures. In order to do so, the school invites eminent academic personalities, researchers and expert educators for guest lectures, presentations, workshops, and knowledge sharing programs who speak for the need of our children relating to international academic standards Kanchanjunga strives for education to meet with.
We are humbled and honored to have earned your trust. We believe in the motto: "Together we can make a difference.” So, we would like to request you to drop in our school on a school day to get a sense of how the mix of our international trained teachers and passionate and qualified local staff has helped to create an unrivalled and conducive learning environment at Kanchanjunga School.
I finally express my sincere gratitude of appreciation to our students, parents, and guardians for trusting us to help you out, and giving us an opportunity to serve you with the best of our education system and services and take this as a note of honor to welcome your gracious visit to Kanchanjunga at your convenience.
Warm Regards,
Padam Bahadur Ayer

(Principal)
